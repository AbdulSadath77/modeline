Fashion

The fashion industry is responsible for designing, creating, and marketing clothing and accessories. It is a global industry that is worth billions of dollars, and it is constantly evolving. If you are interested in a career in fashion, there are many different paths you can take. You could work as a designer, a stylist, a buyer, a marketer, or a sales representative.

Modeling

Modeling is the art of posing for photographs or walking the runway. Models are used to promote clothing, beauty products, and other products. If you are interested in a career in modeling, there are many different types of modeling you can do. You could work as a fashion model, a commercial model, a print model, or a runway model.
